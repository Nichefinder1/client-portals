Our Strategy
Where we're headed and why — for every board member
The Vision
Become the premier family-strengthening nonprofit in Tampa Bay — and a model for what a lean, community-rooted 501(c)(3) can accomplish.
Geographic focus: Tampa Bay / Hillsborough County — with international aspirations as we grow.
Our Model — Two-Generation (Two-Gen)
We serve children and parents at the same time — across 5 program areas simultaneously. Most nonprofits serve one or the other. We serve the whole family.
"iCARE delivers food security, education, career development, health, and family strengthening to children and adults in the same household — creating lasting change at the family level."
This framing is our #1 competitive advantage with funders. Always lead with it.
