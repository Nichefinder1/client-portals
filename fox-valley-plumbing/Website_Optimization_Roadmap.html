Service Page Requirements

Every service page must include:

Content Requirements

  • Word Count: 1,500-2,000 words minimum
  • H1 Tag: Primary keyword + location (e.g., "Emergency Plumbing Services in Elgin, IL")
  • H2-H3 Structure: Minimum 5 sections

Required Sections

  1. Hero Section - Primary keyword in H1, Phone CTA, "Request Service" button
  2. Service Overview (150-250 words) - What the service includes
  3. Signs You Need This Service (200-300 words) - 5-7 warning signs
  4. Our Process (150-200 words) - Step-by-step service delivery
  5. Why Choose Fox Valley (150-200 words) - 15+ years, licensed, 24/7, guarantee
  6. Service Area (100-150 words) - Elgin, South Elgin mention
  7. FAQ Section (5-8 questions) - FAQ schema markup
  8. Related Services - 3-4 internal links
  9. CTA Footer - Phone: (847) 624-3872

Technical Requirements

  • Schema Markup: Service schema + LocalBusiness
  • Internal Links: 3-5 contextual links
  • Images: 2-4 relevant images with alt text
  • Page Speed: <3 seconds load time
  • Mobile: 100% responsive

Location Page Requirements

Every location page must include:

Content Requirements

  • Word Count: 1,500-2,000 words (primary cities), 1,000-1,200 words (secondary)
  • H1 Tag: "Plumber in [City], IL" or "[City] Plumbing Services"

Required Sections

  1. Hero Section - City name in H1, Service area map, Phone CTA
  2. About [City] Services (200-300 words)
  3. Services We Offer in [City] (300-400 words)
  4. Local Landmarks & References (150-200 words)
  5. [City] Plumbing FAQ (5-8 questions with schema)
  6. Customer Reviews from [City]
  7. Why [City] Residents Choose Fox Valley
  8. Service Area Map
  9. CTA Section

Technical SEO Checklist

On-Page SEO

  • ☐ Title Tags: Primary keyword + location + brand (60 chars max)
  • ☐ Meta Descriptions: Include keyword, CTA, phone number (155 chars max)
  • ☐ H1 Tags: One H1 per page with primary keyword
  • ☐ URL Structure: Clean, keyword-rich (e.g., /services/drain-cleaning/)
  • ☐ Image Alt Text: Descriptive, include keywords where natural
  • ☐ Internal Links: 3-5 contextual links per page

Technical Infrastructure

  • ☐ SSL Certificate: HTTPS enabled
  • ☐ Mobile Responsiveness: 100% mobile-friendly
  • ☐ Page Speed: <3 seconds (desktop), <4 seconds (mobile)
  • ☐ XML Sitemap: Updated, submitted to GSC
  • ☐ Canonical Tags: Self-referencing on all pages
